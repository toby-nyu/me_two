Music

I have a large vinyl collection and ran several record labels in the 1990s, where I wrote and produced drum and bass music.

Earlier in my career, I worked as a DJ and radio presenter at various stations in the North East on England

My son also produces music - you can find his music on Spotify, although you probably won't find more than 45 seconds of mine!

In my youth I worked at Midnight Electronics, a company specializing in audio and lighting equipment for events and venues.

I have a strong background in audio engineering and production, which has greatly influenced my teaching methods.

Additionally, my experience in the music industry allows me to bring real-world insights into the classroom.

Theatre

I also worked in theatre, including the Playhouse, Gulbenkian Theatre, and Tyne Theatre & Opera House, handling lighting, sound desks, and creating sound effects.

Midnight Electronics

Midnight Electronics is a Newcastle-based provider of professional audio and visual equipment. They supply equipment to theatres, bands, clubs, and local authorities for a wide range of events and presentations.

Their extensive inventory includes sound systems, microphones, DJ decks, amplifiers, and lighting equipment, all available for hire individually or as part of custom packages.

The company has built a reputation for supporting live productions and installations across the North East, offering reliable equipment and technical expertise to the entertainment and events industry.

Their catalogue is available online, and they serve clients from small community venues to large-scale theatrical productions.
